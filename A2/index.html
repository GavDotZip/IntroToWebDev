About

Eterna City PC is a fictional sports club representing the vibrant community of Eterna City, located in the Sinnoh region. Founded in the spirit of camaraderie and competition, our club strives to promote teamwork, sportsmanship, and excellence both on and off the field.

Our mission is to provide opportunities for local talent to showcase their skills and passion for the beautiful game. We are committed to fostering a supportive environment where players can develop their abilities and forge lasting friendships.

At Eterna City PC, we believe in the power of sports to unite people from diverse backgrounds and inspire positive change. Join us as we embark on an exciting journey to make our mark in the world of Pokemon!
